# ChipList

Shows list of chips and with built-in behavior of show more/less for large lists.

## Props

<Props of={ChipList} />

## Examples

### Default

<Playground>
  <ChipList
    list={[
      "Education",
      "Entertainment",
      "Heath-Advocacy",
      "Heath-Online Communities"
    ]}
    limit={3}
  />
</Playground>
